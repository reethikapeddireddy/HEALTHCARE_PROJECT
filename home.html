AI-Powered Patient Behavior Monitoring System

Intelligent, Real-time Vigilance for Enhanced Patient Safety

Project Aim and Solution

The primary goal of this system is to deploy a **non-invasive, continuous monitoring solution** that significantly improves patient safety. In high-stakes environments, instantaneous detection of critical events—such as sudden falls, seizures, or acute distress—is paramount, especially when medical staff are stretched thin.

This technology leverages advanced **Computer Vision** models. Instead of relying on passive observation, the system actively analyzes video streams to identify abnormal behavior patterns. This reliable differentiation between normal activity and an emergency state ensures that caregivers receive **immediate, actionable audio alerts**, cutting down response time from minutes to mere seconds.

System Architecture Overview

A three-stage process for real-time vigilance.

Video Stream Input
AI Model Analysis & Detection
Instant Audio & UI Alert

Key Advantages of the System

Critical Alert System

Instantly detects high-risk events like **falls and seizures**, providing time-sensitive alerts that minimize the window for potential harm.

24/7 Continuous Vigilance

Ensures tireless monitoring regardless of time or ward volume, offering consistent safety coverage, especially during night shifts.

Enhanced Patient Privacy

Focuses on analyzing movement vectors and generalized body language, prioritizing privacy over intrusive facial recognition.
